Find Your Hair Type

Curly Hair

Define curls and reduce frizz with these techniques:

  • Use sulfate-free shampoos
  • Apply products to soaking wet hair
  • Diffuse instead of towel drying
  • Sleep on silk pillowcases

Fine Hair

Add volume without weighing hair down:

  • Lightweight mousses
  • Root-lifting sprays
  • Dry shampoo at roots
  • Avoid heavy oils/butters

Damaged Hair

Repair chemically treated or heat-damaged hair:

  • Protein treatments weekly
  • Bond-building products
  • Heat protectant always
  • Trim every 6-8 weeks
